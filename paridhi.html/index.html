About Our Department

Education is evolving at a faster pace than any other period in recent history. The curriculum at the Faculty of Computer Science & Applications is aligned as per the industry's requirement to meet tomorrow's challenges for the budding engineers and technocrats. Our dedicated team of faculty members are skilled to solve tough problems, collaborate effectively, and express ideas in innovative ways.

The Faculty comprises of departments — BCA, BCA (Cloud Computing), and BCA (Cyber Security and System Administration). Our curriculum focuses on practical exposure, industry-aligned syllabus, problem-solving, and coding. We use Google Classroom as our LMS with 1000+ videos and study materials. Various MoUs have been established with the industry.

  • Established under M.P.act no.17 of 2015
  • 80+ Companies Visit Campus Yearly for Recruitment
  • Thrust Areas: AI, IoT, Electric Vehicle, Robotics & Automation
  • Google Classroom LMS with 1000+ Videos
Our Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that fosters innovation, cultivates leaders, and inspires a passion for lifelong learning, empowering our diverse community to address the challenges of the 21st century with knowledge, integrity, and commitment to positive societal impact.

Our Mission

Our mission at Mandsaur University is to provide a transformative educational experience that combines academic rigor with practical skills, nurtures a diverse and inclusive community, promotes groundbreaking research, and instills a commitment to ethical leadership and social responsibility. We strive to empower our students, faculty, and staff to contribute meaningfully to local and global communities and drive positive change in a rapidly evolving world.

Our Values

At Mandsaur University, we are committed to nurturing individuals who are not only academically accomplished but also equipped with the essential skills to excel in the evolving world of the 21st century. Our focus extends beyond conventional education to encompass critical competencies such as Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Digital Literacy, Behavioral Skills and Ethical Values. We firmly believe that empathy, effective communication, confidence and discipline are integral to personal and professional success.
